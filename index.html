Background

My love for learning was fueled by my current pursuit of a Master's Degree in Data Analytics and achievement of a Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of New Mexico. Notably, working through a college education allowed me to develop organizational skills, problem solving skills, and a well-developed analytical mindset. Moving on to my professional background, I have been working at FedEx for almost 8 years. I began as a part-time Package Handler moving my way up to an Operations Administrator and eventually found my footing as an Operations Coordinator. While working in Operations, I found a passion for data analytics. In my current position, I analyze delivery metrics, scanning intelligence, and customer satisfaction reports to identify areas of improvement and present suggestions to service providers for enhancing business operations.

As an Operations Coordinator, I analyze delivery metrics using SAP Business Objects, Excel, and PowerBI to build dashboards to pinpoint areas of improvement. I have been polishing my skills in Python and slowly developing my skills in R. During my Master's Degree, I have taken the classes The Data Analytics Journey, Data Acquisition, Data Cleaning, Exploratory Data Analysis, and Predictive Modeling. I have learned statistical principles and how to apply these to answer data driven questions.
